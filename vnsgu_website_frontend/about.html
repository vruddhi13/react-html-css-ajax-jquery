About Our Department

Shaping Future Technologies with Innovation & Excellence

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in Information and Communication Technology, fostering innovation and preparing students to excel in the global IT industry.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education, encourage research, and cultivate professional ethics and technical skills among students to meet the demands of the modern world.

Our History

Established in 2005, the ICT Department at VNSGU has grown into a hub of technological learning and research. Over the years, we have nurtured thousands of students who are now making significant contributions in IT industries worldwide.
